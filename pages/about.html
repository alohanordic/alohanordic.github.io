Aloha nordic was formed in the 2015-16 winter season. We're mainly based in the San Francisco bay area, but our members have roots all across the country. Some of our team members grew up cross country skiing, while others have backgrounds in a variety of sports and recently discovered this sport that we all love. You can find us in our colorful race onesies competing in most of the XC races in Tahoe as we prepare for the Birkie, because we believe that the Birkie is a lifestyle.
