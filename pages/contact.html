To apply for team membership, send a short email, comically worded, addressing, at minimum, the bullet points below to aloha-nordic@googlegroups.com
  • Why are you qualified to be a part of SF’s premier nordic ski team?
  • What sparked your passion for this obscure sport?
  • Is there anything else you are equally passionate about that you care to share (please, don’t be shy)?
  • Do you own roller skis? Why not?
  • What wax technique would you use for the extremely rare snow conditions of slushy powder in San Francisco (a 100yr storm, we hope to experience in our lifetime)
