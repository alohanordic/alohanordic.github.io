To apply for team membership, send a short email addressing, at minimum, the bullet points below to aloha-nordic@googlegroups.com
  • Why are you applying to be an athlete on Aloha Nordic, SF's premier xc ski team?
  • How did you hear about Aloha?
  • Who taught you this obscure sport?
  • What is your biggest life accomplishment so far? Alternatively, please describe your most embarrassing moment.
  • Who's your hero?
  • On a scale from 0 - 10, what is your ability to do the following:
    • Skate ski
    • Classic ski
    • Eat pasta
    • Wear lycra
    • Dance to reggaetone
    • Make conversation in a car with strangers for 3 hours
    • Snore in a crowded bunked room
  • Do you roller-ski, too?
  • Who wore it better?
